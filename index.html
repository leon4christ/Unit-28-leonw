What Is Fake News?

Fake news is one of of the internets biggest issues when it comes to circulation of information. Fake news is any information that is made out to be real, genuine news, but is really illegitimate or unsupported evidentially. There are many ways fake news spreads nowadays, especially considering the large rise in social media usage in the last decade and a half. This is why Stop the Fakery was made -- to help combat this war on legitimate information!
